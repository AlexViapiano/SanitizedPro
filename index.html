About

Founded in 2013, Sanitized Pro is an emerging leader in disaster cleanup and restoration services.

The Company is currently offering its sanitization and decontamination services in Montreal, Toronto, New York and Miami, and is recruiting to expend and all major markets in North America.

COVID-19 RESPONDER

In nearly a century, the world has not experienced a pandemic like the COVID-19. Sanitized Pro and its experts have quickly developed protocols for decontamination and sanitization of COVID-19 exposed areas.

Our revolutionary electrostatic spray technology delivers superior coverage against pathogens and it is safe minutes after completion of sanitization.

Our COVID-19 Responder services not only seeks to eliminate risk of contamination from COVID-19 and other contagions, but provides a reassurance to employees, and consumers that the area has been fully sanitized. Once the COVID-19 Responders have completed the decontamination and sanitization of the space, a certificate of completion is provided and displayed.

It is said that the risk associated to COVID-19 may last for several years and or until an effective treatment and or a vaccine is discovered. For this reason, we offer our continued support to our customers with a daily, weekly or monthly COVID-19 Response service.
